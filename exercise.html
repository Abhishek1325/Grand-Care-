Here are some simple exercises for balance and strength:

  • Strength exercise: Do 10 chair squats (sit and stand up).

Fall Prevention Tips

To prevent falls, make sure to:

  • Remove any tripping hazards in your home (e.g., rugs, cords).
  • Use handrails and non-slip mats in the bathroom.
  • Keep areas well-lit to improve visibility.
